# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 11, 2025

This Privacy Policy ("this Policy") describes how HIDEYUKI OGASAWARA ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and handles your personal information in connection with the app "PawwayNow" ("the App").

---

## Article 1 – Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide

| Type | Details |
|------|---------|
| Account information | Name and email address obtained via Google or Apple Sign-in |
| Dog profile | Your dog's name, breed, birthday, sex, weight, and other details you register |
| Calendar & records | Health logs, activity records, and custom template content |
| Spot proposals | Spot information you submit (name, description, location) |
| Community posts | Event listings and other content you post |

### 2. Information Collected Automatically

| Type | Details |
|------|---------|
| Device information | OS type, version, and device model (for crash analysis) |
| App usage data | Feature usage logs and activity (for service improvement) |
| Crash reports | Error logs and crash reports |
| Advertising identifiers | Ad ID (IDFA/GAID) and IP address (for ad delivery) |
| Push notification token | For delivering push notifications via Firebase Messaging |

### 3. Location Information

When you use the Map or Check-in features, we access your device's location. **Location data is processed only on your device and is never stored on our servers or transmitted externally.** During check-in, location data is used solely to calculate your distance from a spot.

---

## Article 2 – How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

- Providing and operating the App
- Improving service quality and developing new features
- Displaying and managing dog profiles and records
- Providing the Check-in feature (location verification)
- Sending push notifications
- Responding to support inquiries
- Preventing misuse and ensuring security
- Displaying and personalizing advertisements (see Article 4)
- Statistical and analytical purposes (in anonymized form)

---

## Article 3 – Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share your information with third parties except in the following cases:

1. With your consent
2. When required by law
3. When necessary to protect life or property
4. When shared with external services as described in Article 4, within the scope of this Policy

**We do not sell your personal information to third parties.**

## Article 5 – Data Storage and Retention

| Data Type | Storage Location | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Account info & dog profile | Firebase (Google LLC servers) | Until account deletion |
| Calendar & record data | Firebase (Google LLC servers) | Until account deletion |
| Spot proposals & community posts | Firebase (Google LLC servers) | Until account deletion |
| Location data | On-device only (not stored on servers) | Only while using the feature |
| Crash logs | Firebase Crashlytics | Per each service's policy |
| Advertising identifiers | Ad provider servers | Per each service's policy |

When you delete your account, all personal data stored on Firebase will be **immediately and permanently deleted**.

## Article 6 – Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures, including Firebase's built-in security features (authentication, Firestore security rules, etc.), to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, alteration, and leakage. However, no method of data transmission or storage over the internet is completely secure.

---

## Article 7 – Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

1. **Access**: You may request to review the personal information we hold about you.
2. **Correction**: You may request correction of inaccurate information.
3. **Deletion**: You can delete your account from the App's settings at any time, which will immediately and permanently delete all your personal data.
4. **Ad opt-out**: You can disable personalized ads via [Google's ad settings](https://adssettings.google.com).

For requests related to the above, please contact us at the address listed at the end of this Policy.

---

## Article 8 – Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from users under 18. If we become aware that a user under 18 has provided personal information, we will delete it promptly.

---

## Article 9 – Cookies and Similar Technologies

As a native mobile app, PawwayNow does not use browser cookies. However, Firebase and advertising services may use device identifiers and similar technologies to provide, improve, and personalize their services and ads.

---

## Article 10 – Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy as needed to reflect changes in the law or our services. When significant changes are made, we will notify you through in-app notifications or by updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
